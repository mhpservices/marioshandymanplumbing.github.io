About

Mario Wolthers has been a plumber since 1995 and proudly served the Las Vegas, NV area for 20 years. He created Mario’s Handy Plumbing to make sure customers are genuinely happy—every job, every time. Today, our focus is simple: do excellent work, communicate clearly, and stand behind what we install so you can feel confident long after we leave.

How we do business

  • On-time arrivals. Clear arrival windows with real-time updates—no surprises.
  • Upfront pricing. The price you approve is the price you pay.
  • No-pressure options. Choices to fit your needs and budget.
  • Clean, code-compliant installs.
  • Follow-through. Personal check-in to ensure you’re 100% satisfied.
